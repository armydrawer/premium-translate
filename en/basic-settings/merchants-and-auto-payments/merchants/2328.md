## Module Settings

In the admin panel, create a new merchant in the "**Merchants**" ➔ "**Add Merchant**" section.

Select 2328 from the drop-down list in the "**Module**" field, enter a name for the module, and click "**Save**."

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (7).png" alt="" width="339"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Fill in the specified authorization fields.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (1) (1).png" alt="" width="370"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Domain** — leave this field empty.

**Project ID** — the ID you copied earlier from your 2328 account.

**Payment API Key** — the key you copied earlier from your 2328 account.
