## Settings in the Merchant's Personal Account

Register on the [Mocpay](https://mocpay.io/signup) service and log in to your personal account.

## Module Settings

In the admin panel, create a new merchant in the "**Merchants**" section by selecting "**Add Merchant**."

Choose Mocpay from the dropdown menu in the "**Module**" field, enter a name for the module, and click "**Save**."

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/изображение (232).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Fill in the required authorization fields.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/изображение (230).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Domain** — leave this field empty.

**API Key** — enter the API key displayed in the settings of your Mocpay personal account.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/изображение (233).png" alt="" width="527"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Special Fields

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/изображение (231).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Payment Method** — select the appropriate method for providing details to receive funds from the client.
