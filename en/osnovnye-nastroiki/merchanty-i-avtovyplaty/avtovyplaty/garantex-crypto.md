## Merchant Account Settings

Register on the [Garantex website](https://garantex.org/). After your registration is confirmed by the service, log in to your personal account. Enable 2FA (two-factor authentication) for your account.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (641).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once logged in, contact support to request API access for your account.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (640).png" alt="" width="516"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (636).png" alt="" width="325"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

After API access is granted, go to the "Settings" section and create a new API key.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (637).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Save the information highlighted in the box to a text file.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (643).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click "**View / Edit**" next to the created key.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (644).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

To enable payouts through the merchant, activate the "**Withdraw**" method. If you plan to use automatic currency conversion to purchase funds for payouts, also activate the "**Trading**" method.

**Important:** For enhanced security, be sure to enter your server’s IP address in the "IPs" field.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (645).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## **Module Settings**

In the admin panel, create a new merchant under "**Auto Payouts**" -> "**Add Auto Payout**."

Select **Garantex Crypto** from the "**Module**" dropdown, enter a name for the module, and click "**Save**."

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (623).png" alt="" width="474"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Fill in the required authorization fields.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (633).png" alt="" width="431"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

- **Private Key** — the private key generated by Garantex when creating API access (field "**Private Key**")
- **UID** — the access ID generated by Garantex when creating API access (field "**API UID**")

**Host** — select any domain

## Special Fields

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (1480).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* **Purchase missing crypto amount from balance for payout:**  
  • **No** — the payout will be made directly from the balance of the specified currency  
  • **Yes** — if there isn’t enough currency for the payout, the missing amount will be purchased at the market rate and the full amount will be paid out (balance of the payout currency + purchased amount — after the payout, the balance of the payout currency will be zero)  
  • **Purchase but do not withdraw** — the full amount will be purchased at the market rate in the payout currency and kept on the balance (instead of paying out, the balance of the currency will remain the same as before the payout)  
  • **Purchase the entire amount** — the full amount will be purchased at the market rate in the payout currency for subsequent payout (the balance of the specified currency will not be affected)  
* **Determine trade operation currency:**  
  • **Automatically** — the currency used to purchase the payout currency will be selected automatically based on your merchant’s currency balances  
  • **Manually** — the currency specified in the "**Trade Operation Code**" field will be used  
* **Trade Operation Code (if Manual)** — specify the currency to purchase manually (usually USDT)
