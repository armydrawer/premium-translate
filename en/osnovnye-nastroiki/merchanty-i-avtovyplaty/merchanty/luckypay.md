## Settings in Your Account

Register at the [Luckypay](https://luckypay.io/) service. Log in to your account, go to the "**Terminals**" section, select an existing terminal or create a new one, and copy the API key provided in its settings.

## Module Settings

In the admin panel, create a new merchant by going to "**Merchants**" -> "**Add Merchant**."

Select Luckypay from the dropdown menu in the "**Module**" field, enter a name for the module, and click "**Save**."

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (319).png" alt="" width="418"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Fill in the required authorization fields.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Arc_5NJ6WqIhsR (1).png" alt="" width="435"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**API Key** — the key you previously copied from your Luckypay account.
