Register an account with [Super Money](https://platform-v2.moneyhoney.io/) and log in to your merchant dashboard.

## Module Configuration

In the admin panel, create a new merchant by going to "**Merchants**" -> "**Add Merchant**."

Select **Super Money** from the dropdown menu under "**Module**," enter a name for the module, and click "**Save**."

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (1886).png" alt="" width="449"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Fill in the required authorization fields.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (302).png" alt="" width="454"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

- **Client ID** — the key provided to you by your Super Money manager  
- **Client secret** — the secret key provided to you by your Super Money manager  
- **Signature secret** — the signature key provided to you by your Super Money manager  

## Special Fields

**Payment Method** — select the preferred method for receiving funds from your customers:

- **ACCOUNT** — provides a bank account number  
- **CARD** — provides bank card details  
- **SBP** — provides a phone number for receiving payments via the SBP system
